PS4

PS4 is the abbreviation of PlayStation 4. PlayStation 4 is Sony's sixth foray into the video game market and, like the other consoles developed by the company, it integrates more features, options and possibilities than PlayStation 3 or earlier.


But first of all, what is PlayStation 4? PS4 is a digital entertainment system and the fourth desktop console developed by Sony Computer Entertainment. It features a Jaguar x86-64 eight-core APU processor developed by AMD. The GPU also made by AMD Radeon has a processing power of 1.84 Teraflops. PS4 memory has been raised to 8GB DDR5 RAM with a bandwidth of 176GB per second. At the launch of PS4 the console will have a 500GB hard drive, 2 USB 3.0 ports, 1 HDMI port, an auxiliary input, Ethernet port, Wi-Fi connection b / g / n, Bluetooth 2.1 connection and Blu-ray reader 6x and 8x DVD. in 2016 they have released a more powerful evolution called PlayStarion Pro (PS4 Pro) with 1 Tb of storage.
